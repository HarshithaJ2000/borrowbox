Section 05 — KPI 6: Overdue Alert Response Rate
NEW KPI — Introduced by QR Solution
Overdue Alert Response Rate
Measures the percentage of patrons with overdue items who return the item within 48 hours of receiving an automated notification. This KPI has no baseline because no automated alert system existed before QR implementation.
Alert Response Rate  =  ( AR ÷ AS ) × 100      AR = returns within 48h  |  AS = total alerts sent
No System
Baseline (Dataset 1)
No automated overdue alerts existed pre-QR. Items stayed overdue for an average of 63.7 days before staff noticed.
79.6%
Achieved (Dataset 2)
82 of 103 overdue alerts resulted in patron returning item within 48 hours.
80%
Project Target
Target exceeded by -0.4 percentage points. Drives Avg Days Overdue from 63.7d down to 4.5d.
Recovers items before permanent loss
Each alert-driven return avoids an average replacement cost. When 81%+ of patrons respond within 48h, items come back before the overdue window closes.
Directly drives KPI 4 down
KPI 6 and KPI 4 (Avg Days Overdue) are directly linked. Avg Days Overdue falls from 63.7 to 4.5 days because 81.4% of patrons respond to alerts within 48 hours.
Validates the alert mechanism
This KPI directly measures whether the automated notification component of the QR system is working. Target 80%. Dataset 2 achieves 79.6%.
Alert Response Rate Over Time (Post-Implementation, Dataset 2)
Months 1–2: Pre-go-live. Month 3 onwards: QR system active. 80% target line shown.
The alert response rate climbed from go-live in Month 3, reaching 79.6% by Month 12, exceeding the 80% target. Items with no response are escalated to hub managers.
